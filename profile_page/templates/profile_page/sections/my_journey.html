After some time, the company opened a new position as systems engineer. That was a key moment in my life and the direction which I choose. I started to learn design patterns, databases, PHP, javascript, HTML and other web related technologies including linux and docker.

After two years, systems engineering were devided into smaller parts and I become part of RPA Team. There we started with Python. Now I working as Systems Engineering Supervisor. I programming mostly in my free time but python is still my favorite. And what will be next ? We will see ...
