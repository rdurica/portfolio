MY DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY

As a Systems Engineering Supervisor with a background in programming, I have a passion for technology and a drive to constantly improve my skills. My journey began with the use of VBA macros in Excel, which sparked my interest in programming. I quickly expanded my knowledge to include C#, object-oriented programming, and more. Using my programming skills, I have developed several applications that have helped streamline processes for myself and my colleagues.

I had the opportunity to further my career as a Systems Engineer, where I delved into design patterns, databases, web development, Linux and Docker. This led to a specialization in RPA, where I became proficient in Python and took on a leadership role in the team.

As a Systems Engineering Supervisor, I continue to program in my free time and consider Python to be my favorite language. I am excited to see where my passion for technology takes me next.
